Eagle Miles

Frequent Flyer Program

Why do we have Eagle Miles: We have our Eagle Miles program to reward to who consistently take to the skies with us as their go-to choice for regional flights in Texas and surrounding areas.

How to get miles: Miles are earned with every flight taken with Eagle Airlines while a registered member of the Eagle Miles program. One mile is earned for every air mile flown.

How to use miles: Miles are redeemable for free flights with Eagle Airlines. Call 555-USE-MILE and book your flight with the United States based customer service agent
